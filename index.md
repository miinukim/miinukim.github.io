I am **Minwoo (James) Kim**, an M.S. student in **Computer Science & Engineering at Seoul National University**, where I am a member of the **Quantum Information and Quantum Computing Lab** advised by **Prof. Taehyun Kim**. My work focuses on **quantum machine learning**, **quantum information science**, and **quantum computer architecture**, with recent projects spanning hybrid quantum-classical algorithms, quantum reservoir computing, and trapped-ion system software.

I previously received my **B.S. in Physics** from **Seoul National University**, where I wrote my graduation paper on the practical limitations of a Variational Quantum-Neural Hybrid Eigensolver.

## Research and Software

### Quantum Machine Learning for Autonomous Driving

**With Hyundai Motor Group**, 2023-2024

- Investigated quantum machine learning approaches for object detection in autonomous driving.
- Developed simulation pipelines using **Qiskit** and **PyTorch** to study hybrid quantum-classical models.

### Quantum Computer Architecture Design

**2025**

- Designed a high-level architecture for trapped-ion quantum computers, including compilation, scheduling, and data pre/post-processing modules.
- Developed a Python library enabling integration with Qiskit-based workflows for program execution and system access.

### Trapped-Ion Experiment Software Development

**2025-Present**

- Developed an integrated software framework for end-to-end control of trapped-ion experiments, including remote entanglement experiments and surface-trap ion systems.
- The system is currently used for experimental operations across the QuIQCL laboratory.

### Selected Software

- **VQNHELib** (2024-Present): Qiskit/PyTorch implementation of diagonal non-unitary post-processing algorithms introduced in arXiv:2602.17295. Repository: [github.com/miinukim/vqnhelib](https://github.com/miinukim/vqnhelib)
- **NISQRCLib** (2026-Present): Generalized Qiskit-based implementation of the NISQ reservoir computing algorithm proposed in *Nature Communications* **15**, 7491 (2024). Repository: [github.com/miinukim/nisqrclib](https://github.com/miinukim/nisqrclib)
- **BLACKHOLE & PyQCSNU** (2025): Software framework for high-level control of trapped-ion quantum computers, including scheduling, hardware control, and experimental data acquisition. Public client repository: [github.com/snu-quiqcl/pyqcsnu](https://github.com/snu-quiqcl/pyqcsnu)
