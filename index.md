I am **Minwoo (James) Kim**, an M.S. student in **Computer Science & Engineering at Seoul National University**, where I am a member of the **Quantum Information and Quantum Computing Lab** advised by **Prof. Taehyun Kim**.

My research sits at the intersection of:

- quantum machine learning
- quantum information science
- quantum computer architecture

I previously received my **B.S. in Physics** from Seoul National University, where I wrote my graduation paper on the practical training limitations of a **Variational Quantum-Neural Hybrid Eigensolver**.

## Current Work

My recent work includes rigorous hybrid quantum-classical eigensolver methods, Qiskit-based software for NISQ reservoir computing, and system software for trapped-ion quantum computing experiments.

## Research Highlights

- **Publications:** two arXiv preprints on hybrid quantum algorithms and quantum differential equation methods.
- **Talks:** oral and poster presentations at **QISK 2025**, **QISK 2026**, and **IEEE Quantum Week 2025**.
- **Software:** developer of **VQNHELib**, **NISQRCLib**, and trapped-ion control software including **BLACKHOLE** and **PyQCSNU**.
- **Industry collaboration:** worked on quantum machine learning for autonomous driving with **Hyundai Motor Group**.

## Selected Interests

- Variational quantum algorithms
- Quantum-neural hybrid methods
- Quantum reservoir computing
- Trapped-ion quantum systems
- Quantum compilation and architecture
