title: VQNHELib
description: Qiskit and PyTorch implementation of hybrid quantum-classical eigensolver post-processing
---

**VQNHELib** is a software implementation of the diagonal non-unitary post-processing approach introduced in my work on rigorous hybridization of variational quantum eigensolvers and classical neural networks.

## Scope

- Qiskit-based quantum workflow integration
- PyTorch-based classical post-processing modules
- Research code for hybrid eigensolver experiments

## Related Work

- Publication: *A rigorous hybridization of variational quantum eigensolver and classical neural network* (arXiv:2602.17295, 2026)
- Repository: [github.com/miinukim/vqnhelib](https://github.com/miinukim/vqnhelib)
